Our online store is hosted by Shift4Shop. They provide us with the online e-commerce platform that allows us to sell our products and services to you. You can read more about how Shift4Shop uses your Personal Information here https://www.shift4shop.com/privacy.html Payment: If you choose a direct payment gateway to complete your purchase, the online store transmits your credit card data. Your purchase transaction data is stored only as long as is necessary to complete your purchase transaction. After that is complete, your purchase transaction information is deleted. All direct payment gateways adhere to the standards set by PCI-DSS as managed by the PCI Security Standards Council, which is a joint effort of brands like Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover.
